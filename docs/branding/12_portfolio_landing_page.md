[THE PROBLEM]

## Industrial AI fails at the seams

Enterprises don't struggle to train models — they struggle to operate many AI capabilities as one
system. Each new capability arrives with its own data contracts, monitoring, and release process.
Provenance is lost between prediction and action. Degradation goes unnoticed between silos. Every
deployment becomes bespoke risk.

[THE ANSWER]

## Engineer the seams

This platform integrates five AI capability families through one strict rule: **the only thing
that crosses a layer boundary is an immutable, serialisable value object.** No shared mutable
state — which makes the system deterministic, which makes its 51,000-line test suite exact rather
than tolerant.

Underneath runs a full production substrate: model registry with content-addressed artifacts and a
lineage graph; data/concept-drift and model-health monitoring with routed alerts; SLO and
error-budget observability; a 20-gate CI quality engine (itself unit-tested); and a hardened
Docker → Kubernetes deployment chain where liveness, readiness, and rollback are all gated by the
same deterministic health check.
