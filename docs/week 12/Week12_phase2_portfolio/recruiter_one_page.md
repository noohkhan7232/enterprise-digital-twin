# Enterprise Digital Twin & Decision Intelligence Platform
### Recruiter One-Pager

---

**What it is.** An integrated, production-engineered platform that unifies five industrial-AI
capabilities — digital twins, predictive intelligence, agentic AI, retrieval-augmented knowledge
and workflow orchestration — behind one governance, deployment and observability fabric. Built as a
ten-layer architecture over a multi-week engineering programme.

**The problem it solves.** Industrial AI usually fails not because of weak algorithms but because of
weak systems: integration debt, lost provenance, fragmented monitoring and inconsistent governance.
This platform delivers coherence at scale — many capabilities operated together under one model.

---

### Highlights

- **Ten-layer integrated architecture** with a single dependency direction, built additively so no
  layer modifies its predecessors.
- **1,503 deterministic automated tests**, all passing — framework-agnostic, reproducible, no
  external services.
- **~10,620 lines of source across 30 production-engineering modules**, pure Python with a single
  numerical dependency.
- **Full production-engineering substrate**: MLOps with lineage, drift/health monitoring, twenty
  CI/CD quality gates, Kubernetes deployment with zero-downtime rollback, and end-to-end
  observability (metrics, tracing, logging, reliability, SLOs, incidents, capacity).
- **Engineering discipline throughout**: SOLID, dependency injection, immutable domain models,
  determinism, thread safety, composition over inheritance.
