# Enterprise Digital Twin

## Company
GlobalMart Commerce

## Industry
Multi-Warehouse E-Commerce

## Objective

Build a Digital Twin capable of:

- Demand Forecasting
- Inventory Forecasting
- Workforce Forecasting
- Simulation Modeling
- Monte Carlo Risk Analysis
- Optimization
- Decision Intelligence

## Departments

1. Sales
2. Inventory
3. Logistics
4. Suppliers
5. Workforce
6. Customer Service
7. Finance

## Business Questions

- What happens if demand increases by 50%?
- What happens if a supplier fails?
- What happens if shipping costs rise?
- What happens if inventory costs increase?
- What happens if workforce capacity changes?
- What is the optimal business decision?
